Our Foundation

Orchestrating World-Class Vacation & Group Stays

Shankar Travel Agency was founded on a simple concept: travel should be an inspiring experience, free from logistics stress and coordination failures.

We set out to create a high-touch, premium concierge service that handles flight routing, hotel buyouts, local transport, and custom excursion itineraries. Our operations manage corporate retreats, business travel logistics, and luxury honeymoon getaways worldwide.

Today, we work directly with five-star hotel chains and major airlines, bringing exclusive booking rates and seamless service integrity to every traveler who books with us.

Our Operational Values

The standards that guide our travel specialists in delivering a smooth journey.

1. Operational Precision

From double-checking flight connection layovers to confirming resort bed configurations, details matter. We leave nothing to chance.

2. Direct Partner Rates

By establishing direct integrations with global hospitality networks and luxury liners, we bypass intermediary costs to deliver premium value.

3. Round-the-Clock Support

If a flight is cancelled or your schedule shifts mid-trip, our 24/7 client desk is available on WhatsApp and call to re-route your bookings instantly.
